COMPLEXITY ALGORITHM
====================
Score = LOC_points + Files_points + Domain_points + Risk_points + Failure_points

LOC contribution (0-40):
  <50: 0 | <200: 10 | <500: 20 | <1000: 30 | >=1000: 40

Files contribution (0-20):
  <3: 0 | <5: 5 | <10: 10 | >=10: 20

Domain depth (0-15):
  Single domain: 5 | Cross-domain: 10-15

Risk factors (0-25):
  Security impact: 15 | Breaking changes: 10

Historical failure (0-10):
  Based on failure rate 0.0-1.0

Classification:
  score >= 70: CRITICAL
  score >= 45: HIGH
  score >= 20: MEDIUM
  score < 20: LOW

ROLE RECOMMENDATIONS
====================
CRITICAL/HIGH complexity → PRIMARY role (most experienced agent)
Security/breaking changes → CROSS-CHECK role (mandatory review)
MEDIUM complexity → PRIMARY or CROSS-CHECK (hybrid)
LOW complexity → OBSERVER role (learning opportunity)
