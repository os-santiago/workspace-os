## How to Sign

### Individual Contributors

By submitting a Pull Request to Workspace OS, you acknowledge that you have read this CLA and agree to its terms. Your first PR must include a comment stating:

```
I have read and agree to the Contributor License Agreement (CLA.md).
```

### Corporate Contributors

Corporate entities must:

1. Have an authorized signatory send an email to **sergio.canales.e@gmail.com** with:
   - Subject: "WOS Corporate CLA - [Company Name]"
   - Body: "On behalf of [Company Name], I agree to the terms of the Corporate Contributor License Agreement."
   - List of authorized employees (or reference to CORPORATE-CONTRIBUTORS.md PR)

2. Submit a PR adding the company to `CORPORATE-CONTRIBUTORS.md`
